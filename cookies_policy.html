What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files which are stored on your computer, mobile or any other device connected to the internet. Cookies are saved by the browser on Your computer or mobile in accordance with the specified setting of Your browser each time You visit the Website.

Booming Games uses the data from cookies on the basis of its legitimate interests to ensure the proper technical functionality of the Website.

What cookies do we use?

Our Website uses :

  • Strictly necessary cookies: these cookies allow You to navigate on the Website and are indispensable for the operation of the Website or to perform tasks requested by You.
  • Functionality cookies: they are used to recognise You when You return to our Website and enable us to personalise our content for You and remember Your preferences.
  • Targeting or Advertising cookies: these Cookies support the social media sharing functionality. We use an automated tool called Google Analytics which allow Booming Games to records and analyse how visitors use the Website. Google Analytics gains information about websites through the cookies and therefore create reports about the use of our Website.

How to disable cookies?

You can block cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse the collection of all or some cookies. However, if You use browser settings to block all cookies, (including strictly necessary cookies), you may not be able to access all or parts of our Website.

If you do not accept cookies, you can change the cookies preferences of your browser and further information can be found at the following links depending on the browser you are using:

Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en

Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences

Internet Explorer: https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies
